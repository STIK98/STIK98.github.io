About Me

From Physics to Photography! I learned photography from an unlikely source: my background in Physics & Astronomy. A few years ago I finally connected my skills of capturing collosal dim objects milions of miles away with a telescope, to capturing live's precious moments on earth. Understanding light and optics has given me a unique perspective on capturing stunning images, and gives me a deeper understanding of the world around us.
